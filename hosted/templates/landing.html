The mechanism

Five modes, matched to you

Your profile tells the AI which mode to use for which kind of work, so it gets out of your way when you are expert and slows you down on purpose when the thinking is yours to do.

  • Automate: AI executes and annotates. Low friction, mechanical work.
  • Augment: AI accelerates and challenges you in your expert domains.
  • Coach: AI scaffolds and questions. For skills you are actively developing.
  • Protect: AI forces cognition to keep at-risk skills sharp.
  • Hands-off: AI surfaces the decision and lets you make the call.

Does it work with the AI I already pay for?

Yes. Claude, Claude Code, ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, Codex, and any MCP-aware client. One profile follows you across all of them, so you are not locked into a single vendor.

Is it free?

The 3-minute demo needs no sign-up. The full profile, dashboard and coaching come with a 30-day free trial, no credit card. After that it is a flat monthly price, billed only if you choose to continue.

Will the coaching slow me down?

A little, on purpose, and only where it matters. The added time is small, and far less than people say they would accept in exchange for getting better at the work. When you just need speed, switch to ship mode for that task.

What happens to my data?

Your profile and lightweight usage signals are stored so coaching can adapt. You can export or permanently delete everything at any time. Source-available, GDPR-ready, with a self-host option. See the privacy policy.

Can my company deploy it?

Yes. Enterprise gets aggregate-only dashboards (no individual surveillance), SSO, audit logging, self-hosting and EU residency options. Talk to us.
